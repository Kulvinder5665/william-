We Pride Ourselves As A Spices Company That Provides Total Solution

Traderston General Trading Company in UAE began modestly and grew to become one of the most reliable foodstuff trading companies in UAE, dedicated to selling high-quality food products to customers in the country and across the region.
Traderston product portfolio encompasses a range of renowned brands we specialize in rice, grains, tea, and spices from different parts of the world. We have expanded our presence by service premier supermarkets, 5-star hotels, reexport markets, and ship chandlers.
We have taken great strides in creating HACCP compliant system for our processing facilities, ensuring the safe free flow of products throughout. We adhere to strict business ethics in all our operations and comply with international service standards.
Our goal is one- to provide the best foodstuff products that exceed the expectations of our customers. We speak quality, affordability, and availability.
